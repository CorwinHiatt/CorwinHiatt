## About Me

I'm **Corwin Hiatt** — a software architect, founder of **Colee Software Services LLC**, and a builder who genuinely loves writing code. I design and ship fast, accessible, search-optimized systems for clients who care about performance, not just appearances.

I think in **systems, not screens**. Most of my work starts with the same question: *what is this product actually trying to do for the business?* Then I architect around that — data model first, performance budget second, UI last. I prefer opinionated stacks, strong typing, clean contracts between services, and shipping software that holds up under real traffic.

I built Colee Software Services to give clients an engineering partner that operates like an in-house team without the overhead. We're small, deliberate, and near capacity — if you're interested in working with us, reach out early.

---

## How I Build

- **Architecture-first.** I model the domain, define the contracts, and pick the stack to fit the problem — not the other way around.
- **Performance is a feature.** Core Web Vitals, edge caching, and a real performance budget on every build.
- **Accessibility is non-negotiable.** WCAG 2.2 AA / ADA compliance baked in from day one, not bolted on at the end.
- **SEO & AEO by design.** Schema, structured data, semantic HTML, and content architecture engineered for both Google and AI answer engines.
- **Automate everything boring.** MCP servers, custom agents, and pipelines that turn repeatable work into one-click workflows.
- **Ship fast, ship clean.** Typed end-to-end, tested where it matters, and deployed on infrastructure I trust.
- **Compliance as a constraint, not an afterthought.** HIPAA, TCPA, SEC/FINRA investment advisor advertising rules, and ADA baked into architecture decisions — not reviewed after the fact.

## Compliance I Build To

Every regulated engagement is scoped and built against the rules that govern it — not bolted on after the fact.

| Regulation | Who It Applies To | What It Means in Practice |
|------------|-------------------|---------------------------|
| **HIPAA** | Healthcare clients (MC Aesthetics, health-adjacent platforms) | No PHI through the web layer, privacy-respecting analytics, compliant contact and booking flows, business associate agreement awareness |
| **TCPA** | Lead generation, SMS/email outreach pipelines (Allstate, insurance) | Explicit written consent mechanisms, timestamped opt-in records, do-not-call scrubbing workflows, no autodialed or prerecorded contact without consent |
| **SEC Rule 206(4)-1 / FINRA** | Registered investment advisors (Cardinal Wealth Management) | No performance claims or implied returns, Form ADV disclosure linking, testimonial-free copy, advertising-rule-aware language throughout |
| **ADA / WCAG 2.2 AA** | All client builds | Accessible markup, keyboard navigation, screen reader compatibility, contrast compliance — by default, not by request |
| **OSINT / Digital Forensics Standards** | Investigative and nonprofit engagements | Documented methodology, chain of custody, defensible sourcing, findings formatted for legal handoff where applicable |
